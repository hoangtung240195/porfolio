INTERACTIVE GARMENT

JACQUARD

A Google ATAP project, Jacquard weaves conductive threads into everyday clothes, augmenting them to be touch-interactive. The first Jacquard enabled garment is a jacket created in partnership with Levi's.

SMART CUP

PRYME VESSYL

Pryme Vessyl is an intelligent cup that automatically tracks water consumption and hydration levels to help you stay hydrated throughout the day. The cup syncs with an app to provide insights and allow users to learn about their behavior.

HEALTH & TECH BRAND

MARK ONE

Mark One is a consumer brand aiming to build desirable products that inform and inspire people to make healthier choices. The first product, Vessyl, is a cup that identifies, tracks, and aggregates the nutritional value of all beverages.

SOCIAL CONTENT CREATION APP

CINEMAGRAM

Cinemagram is a social app that lets you create and share cinemagraphs from your mobile phone. After filming a short video, painting over parts of the video lets you choose what moves.

NYC SUBWAY CARD REDESIGN

METROCARD

Out of the numerous swiping problems that exist at turnstiles, this conceptual redesign of New York City's iconic MetroCard tackles the specific issue of people swiping their cards in the wrong direction.

CANDY PACKAGING

MMONSTER

Mmonster is a concept candy brand that comes in four colorful flavors: creamy, minty, sour, fruity. These prototypes are printed and constructed individually by hand.

INFOGRAPHIC CALENDAR

HELIOCENTRIC

This graphic calendar depicts a single earth rotation around the sun – one year. It provides dates for solar, planetary, lunar, and meteoric events and depicts accurate scales of the earth, moon, and sun relative to one another.
